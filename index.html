About the Book

THE FRAMEWORK

The way I see the market — built one chart at a time,
refined through everything that didn't work.

This isn't a system I'm telling you to follow. It's the structural approach I've built for myself over a decade of trial, error, and slow correction — and it's the lens I use every day when I sit down to trade.

The Framework is chart-first, indicators second, macro context always. It's how I think when the noise clears. I wrote it for the trader who's tired of guru content that assumes you already know everything — written in language that meets you where you are, without pretending I've got everything figured out either.

You won't find signals here. You won't find shortcuts or promises. What you'll find is one trader's way of looking at the chart, the rules I follow to keep myself disciplined, and the thinking behind why I built it this way. If any of that helps you find your own way of seeing, that's the point.
